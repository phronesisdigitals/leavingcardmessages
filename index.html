Writing tips

✏️

Add one personal line

Even a generic message becomes memorable with a single specific detail — a shared memory, or their name.

📏

Shorter is usually better

Two well-chosen sentences beat a paragraph of fillers. Card space is limited — make every word count.

🎭

Match the relationship

A heartfelt message for someone you barely know reads oddly. Stick to warm-but-professional if in doubt.

🔄

Try all three options

We generate three variations every time. Read all of them before copying — the third is often the best.
