About LeavingCardMessages

The free tool that takes the blank-page panic out of signing a card.

Why we built this

You know the moment. Someone passes around an office card — leaving, retirement, new baby — and when it reaches you, your mind goes completely blank. You want to write something genuine, but "Best wishes, [name]" feels like a cop-out, and you've got about thirty seconds before everyone's looking at you.

LeavingCardMessages was built for exactly that moment. It's a simple, free AI tool that turns a few quick details about the person and occasion into three ready-to-use card messages — warm, personalised, and written in proper UK English. No sign-up, no faff, no subscription.

We cover different card types: leaving, birthday, sympathy, congratulations, retirement, thank you, new baby, and get well. Each one asks just a handful of questions to make the output feel personal rather than generic.

How it works

The tool is powered by AI to generate your messages. When you hit "Generate", your choices are sent securely to the API, which produces three tailored options in a few seconds. We don't store what you type, and you don't need an account.

The messages are suggestions, not scripts. They work best when you treat them as a starting point — read all three, pick the one that feels right, and add your own name at the end. Even a small tweak makes it yours.
