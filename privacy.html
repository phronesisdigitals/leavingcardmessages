Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

LeavingCardMessages ("we", "us", "our") operates the website leavingcardmessages.com — a free AI-powered tool that helps people write personalised card messages for workplace and personal occasions. This policy explains what data we collect when you use the site, how we use it, and your rights under UK data protection law (UK GDPR).

You can contact us at hello@leavingcardmessages.com.

What information we collect

Information you enter into the tool: When you use the message generator, you may enter details such as the recipient's relationship to you, a tone preference, and optional personal details (e.g. a shared memory or nickname). We do not store this information on our servers once your session ends. It is sent temporarily to an AI API to generate your message and is not retained.

Usage data: We collect anonymous information about how the site is used, including pages visited, time on site, and browser/device type. This is collected via Google Analytics and is used to understand how we can improve the site.

Cookies: We use cookies as described in the section below.

Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function correctly, including remembering your cookie consent preference. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics): Help us understand how visitors use the site — for example, which message types are most popular. These are only set if you accept cookies.
  • Advertising cookies (Google AdSense): Used to serve relevant ads. Google may use these to personalise the ads you see across the web. These are only set if you accept cookies.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and reloading the page, which will show the consent banner again. You can also manage cookies via your browser settings.

How we use your information

  • To generate card messages in response to your inputs (processed in real time, not stored)
  • To understand site usage and improve the tool (analytics)
  • To display relevant advertisements (if you have accepted advertising cookies)
  • To respond to any queries you send us by email

AI processing

The text inputs you enter to generate messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. Anthropic is a third-party AI provider. The inputs are used solely to generate your requested messages and are not used by us to identify you. We encourage you not to enter sensitive personal information (such as full names, addresses, or confidential details) into the tool.

Anthropic's own privacy policy governs how they handle API data. You can find it at anthropic.com/privacy.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services which may process data about your visit:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis (Google LLC, USA)
  • Google AdSense — advertising (Google LLC, USA)
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI message generation (Anthropic PBC, USA)
  • Netlify — website hosting (Netlify Inc., USA)

Where data is transferred outside the UK (to the USA), it is done so under appropriate safeguards recognised by UK law.

Data retention

We do not store the text you enter into the generator. Analytics data is retained by Google in accordance with their data retention settings. Email correspondence is retained for up to 12 months.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing or withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@leavingcardmessages.com.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
